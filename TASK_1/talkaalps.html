PASSPORT SEVA KENDRA

Tatkaal Passport Service

If you need your passport urgently, you can apply for the Tatkaal Passport Service at the Passport Seva Kendra. This service is available for both fresh and re-issue of passport applications.

Eligibility

To be eligible for the Tatkaal Passport Service, you must meet one of the following criteria:

  • You have a genuine emergency like medical emergency, death in family, etc.
  • You have to travel urgently due to unforeseen circumstances like business travel, study abroad, etc.
  • You have a short validity passport due to expiration or exhaustion of visa pages.

Application Process

The application process for the Tatkaal Passport Service is similar to the regular passport application process, with some additional requirements:

  1. Fill out the online application form and submit it.
  2. Book an appointment at the Passport Seva Kendra for the Tatkaal Passport Service.
  3. Pay the fees online.
  4. Bring all the required documents, including the additional documents required for Tatkaal Passport Service, to the Passport Seva Kendra.
  5. Complete the biometric and photograph capture process.

Documents Required

In addition to the regular passport application documents, you will need to provide the following additional documents for the Tatkaal Passport Service:

  • Verification certificate as per Annexure F
  • Prior Intimation Letter (only for government servants)
  • Identity proof with photocopy of the same
  • Proof of travel
